In 1970 Saburo, Sasai's son, created the Arasaka Security division of the Arasaka Corporation. The new division specialized in high quality personal and corporate manpower, electronic, and computer security and protection. Slowly, over 25 years, Arasaka Security developed a sterling reputation as the most potent company of its type in the world.
